Hun Sen Recovers from COVID-19, Resumes Official Duties

Published: March 4, 2025 - by News Reporter

Prime Minister Hun Sen has recovered from COVID-19 and has resumed his official duties. He tested positive for the virus while attending the G20 summit in Indonesia but has since tested negative and completed his quarantine period.

During his recovery, the Prime Minister continued to monitor the situation in Cambodia and urged citizens to remain vigilant in following health guidelines to prevent the spread of the virus.

He emphasized the importance of vaccination and encouraged those who have not yet been vaccinated to do so promptly.
